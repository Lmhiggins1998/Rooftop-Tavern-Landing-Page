About Us

Rooftop Tavern has a well-earned reputation as Atlanta's most-celebrated home restaurant since opening in 2016, and consistently gains ratings as one of Atlanta's top restaurants. Featuring Atlanta cuisine Gurus and our Owners Marcus & Alexis Rivers, Rooftop Tavern's menu relies entirely on fresh-homegrown ingredients. It's friendly environment serves as a comfort for any person stopping by for one of our delicious meals! Stop by and ask about our "first-timer" deal and our exclusive random deals that will make you want to eat all day long!
